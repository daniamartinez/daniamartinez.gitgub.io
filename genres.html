Different Genres

The art of making music goes as far back as 35,000 years ago, which means as of now there are over 1,300 different genres of music. There are about 10 main genres which branch out and streaming services like Spotify have about 365 million users most listening to at least 3 different genres.

Why are genres important?

Not only do genres illustrate your characteristics, they effect your thoughts and ideas through the brain. Your auditory cortex, amigdala, mesolimbic system, and occipital lobe all become effected based on the different genre of music you play. A prime example is a 2007 study that demonstrated how listening to classical music can help your brain retain information a lot easier.

The 10 generic genres:

Rock, Jazz, EDM, Techno, R&B, Counrty, Pop, Classical, Blues, Traditional
