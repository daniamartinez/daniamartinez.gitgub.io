Songs around the world

  • Songs from North America

    North America has a variety of genres and genres like Pop, Country, and Rap are one of the most popular ones.

    Some recent big hits in those genres include: "As it Was" by Harry Styles (pop), "I hope" by Gabby Barrett (Country), and "Walk Em Down" by Metro Boomin & 21 Savage

  • Songs from South America

    South America is home to many popular genres like Salsa, Cumbia, and Reggaeton.

    Some recent big hits in those genres include: "Mala" by Marc Anthony (salsa), "Cumbia A La Gente" by Guaynaa (cumbia), and "Me Porto Bonito" by Bad Bunny

  • Songs from Europe

    Some of Europes most famous classics are Led Zeppelin, the Beatles and other artists include: Ed Sheeran (british and pop music), Anatoliy Solovianenko (ukrainian and opera singer), and Edith Paif (frech and ballad singer)

  • Songs from Asia

    Asia is home to one of the biggest genres of the world like K-Pop, J-Rock, and traditional classic music.

    One of Aisa's biggest breakthroughs in the music industry was because of a K-Pop band called "BTS" who is the most streamed group of all time. One of Asias most popular song of the 2000's is "Butter" by BTS.

  • Songs from Africa

    Africa's music and culture is the base of many of the genres we know today like rap, jazz, hip-pop. Other genres that are heavily influenced by Africa's culture is; Soukous, Afrobeats, Rhumba, Bongo, and Reggae.

    Some popular songs with Afrobeats include: "Ghost" by Kamo Mphela, "Abo Mvelo" by Daliwonga and "Jagele" by Burna Boy
